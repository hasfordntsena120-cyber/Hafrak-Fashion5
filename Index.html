About Us

Our Story

Located in the heart of Takoradi Fijai, Hafrak Fashion was born out of a passion for authentic African style. We blend traditional techniques with modern trends to create masterpieces for both men and women.

Whether it's the perfect kente gown or a sharp corporate suit, our goal is to bring your vision to life with precision and grace. With years of experience in custom tailoring, alterations, and wedding dress design, we take pride in delivering exceptional quality and personalized service.

Why Choose Us

Personalized Designs

Every client receives a unique consultation. We sketch, tailor, and perfect designs that reflect your personality and cultural pride.

Expert Tailoring & Alterations

Over a decade of precision work: bespoke suits, delicate finishes, and flawless alterations — all completed in-house with care.

Quality Craftsmanship

Only the finest African and European textiles. Every stitch is long-lasting, comfortable, and visually stunning.

Local Expertise With Personal Touch

Based in Fijai, Takoradi, we understand local fashion needs — from traditional ceremonies to corporate events.

Yes, we schedule at least two fittings for custom garments to ensure a flawless finish.
Can I bring my own fabric?
Absolutely! We also provide premium Ankara, Kente, and lace if needed.
